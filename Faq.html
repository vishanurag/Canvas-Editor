Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Creative Canvas Tool and its features.

General Questions

What is Creative Canvas Tool?
Creative Canvas Tool is an intuitive online platform designed for digital artists of all levels. It offers a wide range of tools and features to create, edit, and enhance digital artwork, from basic sketches to complex illustrations.
Is Creative Canvas Tool free to use?
We offer a free tier with essential features, perfect for beginners and casual users. For advanced tools, larger canvas sizes, and premium brushes, we have various subscription plans available.
Do I need to download any software?
No! Creative Canvas Tool is a web-based application, meaning you can access it directly from your browser without any downloads or installations. Just open your browser, go to our website, and start creating.

Technical Questions

What file formats does Creative Canvas Tool support?
Creative Canvas Tool supports popular image formats like JPEG, PNG, GIF, and TIFF for import and export. You can also save your projects in our native .CCT (Creative Canvas Tool) format for future editing.
Is there a mobile version of Creative Canvas Tool?
While Creative Canvas Tool is optimized for desktop browsers, it is responsive and can be accessed on tablets and modern smartphones. A dedicated mobile app is currently under development and will be released soon!
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
We highly value user feedback! You can report bugs or suggest new features through our dedicated "Feedback" page, or by contacting our support team directly via email.
